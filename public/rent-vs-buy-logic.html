The Financial Logic of Renting vs. Buying

The debate between renting and buying is a pillar of personal finance. To evaluate the choice accurately, one must look past the monthly cash outlay and focus on Net Future Value (NFV).

Buying is an investment in a leveraged asset with potential appreciation and tax advantages. Renting offers capital liquidity and eliminates 'Sunk Costs' like maintenance and insurance. The 'Breakeven Point' is determined by how long you stay in the home relative to the returns you could have earned by investing your down payment elsewhere.
